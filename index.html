Meet the Legends

---- In the world of Apex Legends, the story takes place about 30 years after the Titanfall series. Humanity has expanded across the stars using jump drive technology, but conflict between the Core Systems and the Frontier has torn the worlds apart. The long Border Wars left the Frontier shattered and lawless. To bring some order—and profit—out of the chaos, the Apex Games were born: a bloodsport where competitors fight for fame, fortune, and survival.

---- In Apex Legends, there are many Legends, each with their own background and story. As the saying goes, “Legends are a group of outlaws, soldiers, misfits, and misanthropes, each with their own set of skills. The Apex Games welcome all comers — survive long enough, and they call you a Legend.” In the collection below, you can explore each Legend — learn their names, see their pictures, listen to their voices, and click the links for more detailed information.

Legends

Alter

  • Void Breacher

Alter is a reality-bending traveler who slips between dimensions for her own amusement. With her Void tech and a taste for chaos, she’s made a life of stealing, fighting, and double-crossing anyone who gets in her way. Drawn to worlds on the brink of collapse, Alter takes pleasure in witnessing destruction—believing that only in those moments do people show who they truly are. But across countless ruined worlds, one truth keeps haunting her: many of them were destroyed by the same person.
